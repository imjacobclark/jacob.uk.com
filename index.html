About me

Experienced technologist and polyglot software engineer currently working at the BBC.

I specialise in leading and mentoring teams that craft high quality software which meets user needs through a variety of extreme programming and agile techniques.

I strongly believe in building autonomous teams around agile principles, thin slicing, incremental and continuous delivery, clean & test driven code, refactoring, automation, simplicity, and pragmatism.

I have a wide range of public and private industry experience in travel, leisure, broadcast media, government, eCommerce, retail, co-operative and digital agencies.
